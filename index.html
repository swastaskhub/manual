Getting Started

Joining Business Hub is straightforward. Complete registration once and you'll have access to your member dashboard immediately.

1
Visit the platform homepage and select Register.
2
Fill in all required fields accurately — incomplete registrations cannot be processed.
3
Create a secure username and password you will remember.
4
Review your details and submit the registration form.
5
Sign in with your new credentials to access your dashboard.

You will be asked to provide:

  • Full legal name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Country of residence
  • Username and password
ℹ️
Members are responsible for ensuring all provided information is accurate and kept up to date.

05

Completing Tasks

Business Hub offers a variety of task types — including writing, email replies, research, review questionnaires, and more. Each task must be completed fully and carefully before submission.

1
Open the Task Center from your dashboard and browse available assignments.
2
Select a task to open it. You will see two options: View and Submit. Click View to read the full task brief and instructions.
3
Work on the assignment as instructed. This may include writing a response, completing a questionnaire, conducting research, or drafting an email reply — depending on the task type.
4
Once your work is ready, click Submit from your dashboard. This will direct you to the designated submission email address.
5
Send your completed work to that email address. You may attach it as a document or paste the content directly into the email body.
🚫
Images are not accepted. Submit your work as a typed document or pasted text only. Submissions sent as images or screenshots will not be processed.

Best practices for better approval rates:

  • Follow instructions precisely — even small deviations can cause rejection
  • Submit complete information including all required content
  • Avoid duplicate submissions
  • Meet task deadlines where applicable
  • Always send work as a document or typed text, never as an image
⏱️
Only one task can be worked on at a time. After you submit a task, you must wait for the review outcome before a new task becomes available.
06

Submitting Your Work

After completing your task, follow the submission process below to get your work reviewed and your earnings released.

✍️
Complete the Task Finish the assignment in full according to the task instructions — writing, research, questionnaire, or email reply.
📤
Click Submit on Your Dashboard From the task on your dashboard, click the Submit button. You will be directed to the official submission email address.
📧
Send Your Work via Email Compose an email to the address shown and either paste your completed work into the email body, or attach it as a document. Images are not accepted.
🔍
Wait for Review (30 min – 1 hour) Once your submission is received, our review team will assess it. Review typically takes between 30 minutes and 1 hour. Keep an eye on your email during this time.
✅
Receive Your Review Outcome The result will be sent to your email. If approved, you will be prompted to initiate your withdrawal. If corrections are needed, your work will be returned to you.
🔁 If Corrections Are Required

If your submission does not meet the required standard, it will be sent back to your email with specific correction notes. Review the feedback carefully, make the necessary corrections, and resubmit the corrected work to the same email address. Your work will then go through the review process again. Once it passes, you will be prompted to withdraw your earnings.

📬
Monitor your email closely after every submission. Review results and correction requests are sent directly to your registered email address. Delays in responding to correction requests may affect your earnings timeline.

09

Withdrawal Process

Withdrawals are prompted via email once your submitted task has been reviewed and approved. Before initiating a withdrawal, confirm the following:

  • Your account is active
  • You have received an approval email for your submitted task
  • Earnings appear in your available balance
  • Your registered phone number and National ID number are accurate and current
1
Receive Approval Email — After your task is reviewed and approved, you will be prompted via email to initiate your withdrawal.
2
Access Withdrawals — Open the Withdraw section from your dashboard.
3
Enter Required Information — Provide your registered phone number and National ID number, exactly as used during registration, along with the withdrawal amount.
4
Verify Information — Review your phone number and ID number carefully. The name linked to both must match your account name, since the payout goes directly to that M-Pesa line.
5
Submit Request — Once submitted, your withdrawal is processed promptly and paid directly to your M-Pesa.
⚠️
Your phone number and ID number must belong to the same person and match your registered account name. Mismatched details will delay or block your payout.
09b

Withdrawal & International Clients

If you are based outside Kenya or do not have access to M-Pesa, you can withdraw your earnings via Bank Transfer (Wire). The withdrawal option is available directly from your dashboard sidebar.

1
Open the Sidebar — From your dashboard, click the sidebar menu. You will see a withdrawal option listed there.
2
Select Bank Transfer (Wire) — Click the withdrawal option and choose Bank Transfer (Wire) as your payment method.
3
Enter Your Bank Details — Provide your full bank account name, account number, bank name, SWIFT/BIC code, and country. Ensure all details are accurate — errors will delay your payment.
4
Enter the Withdrawal Amount — Confirm the amount you wish to withdraw. It must not exceed your available balance.
5
Submit Your Request — Review all details carefully, then submit. Your request will go through verification before the transfer is processed.
🌍
International bank transfers may take longer to process than M-Pesa payouts due to inter-bank clearing times. Ensure your bank details are fully correct before submitting — incorrect SWIFT codes or account numbers will cause significant delays.
⚠️
The bank account name must match the name registered on your Business Hub account. Transfers to mismatched or third-party accounts are not permitted.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my completed task? ▼
Once you have finished working on your task, click the Submit button on your dashboard. You will be directed to a designated email address. Send your completed work to that email either by pasting it in the email body or attaching it as a document. Images are not accepted.
How long does the review take after I submit? ▼
Review typically takes between 30 minutes and 1 hour after your work is received via email. Stay by your email during this window, as the outcome — whether approval or a correction request — will be sent there.
What happens if my work is sent back for corrections? ▼
You will receive an email with the specific corrections required. Make the necessary changes to your work and resubmit it to the same email address. The review team will assess it again, and once it is approved you will be prompted to initiate your withdrawal.
When can I withdraw my earnings? ▼
Withdrawals become available once your account is active and your submitted task has been reviewed and approved. You will receive an email notification prompting you to initiate the withdrawal. Payouts are made directly to your registered M-Pesa number.
Can I send my work as a photo or screenshot? ▼
No. The platform does not accept image-based submissions. You must submit your work as typed text in the email body or as an attached document (e.g. PDF or Word file).
How do I upgrade to Premium? ▼
Visit the Premium section within your dashboard and follow the upgrade instructions provided. Select a package, complete the payment through an approved method, and your account will be upgraded automatically upon confirmation.
I am an international client without M-Pesa. How do I withdraw? ▼
International members who do not have M-Pesa can withdraw via Bank Transfer (Wire). Open your dashboard sidebar, click the withdrawal option, and select Bank Transfer as your payment method. You will be asked to provide your bank account name, account number, bank name, SWIFT/BIC code, and country. The account name must match your registered Business Hub name. Transfers may take longer than M-Pesa payouts due to inter-bank processing times.
Can I use someone else's payment details? ▼
No. The phone number and National ID number used for withdrawal must belong to you and match your account name exactly, since funds are paid directly to that M-Pesa line.
How do I know my account is activated? ▼
Your dashboard account status will clearly display as Active after successful activation verification. If it still shows inactive after payment, contact support through official channels.
